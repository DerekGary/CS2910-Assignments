Derek's Tips for Student Success



The information below serves as a guide for my own personal success as a college student. As this course progresses, I may create a personal student success guide.


Summary of Information

  1. Get at least 8 hours of rest per night.
  2. Read any notes, course slides, and textbook chapters right before and after class
  3. Attend office hours if you are ever stuck or need guidance.
  4. Create due dates on your phone's calendar app to keep you on track and on time
